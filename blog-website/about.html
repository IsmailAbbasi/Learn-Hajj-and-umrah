About Hajj & Umrah Live

Learn about our mission to spread knowledge and guidance about Hajj and Umrah

Our Story

Hajj & Umrah Live was founded with a clear mission: to provide accurate, accessible, and comprehensive information about the sacred Islamic pilgrimages of Hajj and Umrah. Our journey began when we noticed a gap in the availability of reliable resources that both newcomers and experienced pilgrims could turn to.

What started as a small collection of articles has grown into a trusted resource for Muslims worldwide. We work with Islamic scholars, experienced Hajj guides, and educational institutions to ensure that our content is not only accurate but also presented in a way that's easy to understand.

Our mission is to provide accurate and accessible information about Hajj and Umrah to Muslims worldwide. We strive to make the journey of pilgrimage easier to understand and follow, enabling pilgrims to focus on the spiritual aspects of these sacred rituals.
